Synopsis

The Iron Lung movie is a science-fiction, cosmic-horror film written, directed, and starring YouTuber Mark Fischbach (Markiplier). Based on the 2022 indie video game by David Szymanski, the plot follows a convicted criminal who is welded inside a claustrophobic submarine and forced to pilot it through an alien ocean of blood on a desolate moon.

Movie Summary

In the film Iron Lung, humanity is struggling to survive after a mysterious event called the Quiet Rapture causes all stars and habitable planets to disappear. A prisoner named Simon is sent on a dangerous mission in a small submarine called the Iron Lung to explore a moon covered by an ocean of blood in exchange for freedom. As he ventures deeper, he discovers evidence of previous failed expeditions and realizes a terrifying creature lurks beneath the surface. The mission turns into a fight for survival, ending in a tragic confrontation that reveals more about the horrors hidden within the blood ocean.
